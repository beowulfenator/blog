---

The bakelite knob is much too short and thin for my hands. Clearly, I need to make one that is bigger. I am using the knob from the #40 as inspiration. The hardest part, of course, will be making the knob without a lathe.

!["Knob blank"](/assets/images/2022-03-29-scrub-plane-makeover-2/knob-blank.jpg)

Without a drill press, I don't really trust myself to drill a perpendicular hole. The plan is to drill from each side for the holes to meet in the middle. Besides, it would be much harder to widen the hole once it's drilled.

!["Brace and auger bits"](/assets/images/2022-03-29-scrub-plane-makeover-2/drill.jpg)

The brace drill and some auger bits will come in handy.

I am then going to round the blank using chisels or maybe a spokeshave, then shape it a bit with the rasp. Once that is done, I can mount the knob on some arbor (a bolt, or maybe a piece of allthread), put it into my hand drill and smooth it with sandpaper.
